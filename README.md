This is a guitar game where random chords flash on your screen, prompting you to play said chord on your actual guitar. The app will take in audio and determine whether you played the chord correctly or not. The frontend does provisional scoring that quickly determines whether your playing is correct or not. To deter cheating and ensure fair scoring, a separate Python service is then used, behind a queue, to give an authoritative score for leaderboard purposes. Spring Boot is used as the orchestrator.
